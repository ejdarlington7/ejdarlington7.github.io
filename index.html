About Hawaii

Hawaii is a state of the United States located in the Pacific Ocean. It is composed of eight major islands and numerous smaller islands and atolls. Hawaii is known for its beautiful beaches, tropical climate, and diverse culture.

  • Kahoolawe - "The Target Isle"
  • Kauai - "The Garden Isle"
  • Lanai - "The Pineapple Isle"
  • Maui - "The Valley Isle"
  • Molokai - "The Enlightenment Isle"
  • Niihau - "The Forbidden Isle"
  • Oahu - "The Gathering Place"
