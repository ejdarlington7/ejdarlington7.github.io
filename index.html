About Hawaii

Hawaii is a state of the United States located in the Pacific Ocean, approximately 2,000 miles from the mainland. It is composed of eight major islands. The state capital is Honolulu, which is located on the island of Oahu. Hawaii is known for its unique scenery and natural beauty. The landscape in Hawaii is composed of towering mountains, rich rainforests, white and black sand beaches, and crystal clear waters. It is a very popular destination for all kinds of water sports and for tourists as well. There is something for everyone to enjoy in Hsawaii. Learn more about Hawaii at Hawaii's Wikipedia Page!
