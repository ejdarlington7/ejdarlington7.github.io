Oahu Island

Oahu is the third largest island in Hawaii and is home to the state capital of Honolulu. It is known for its beautiful beaches, historical landmarks, and bustling nightlife.

Some popular attractions on Oahu include Waikiki Beach, Pearl Harbor, Diamond Head, and the Polynesian Cultural Center.
