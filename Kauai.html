About Kauai

Kauai is the fourth-largest island in Hawaii and is known for its rugged natural beauty, including towering cliffs, lush valleys, and cascading waterfalls. Kauai is also home to the iconic Na Pali Coast.

Things to Do in Kauai

  • Hikingthe Kalalau Trail on the Na Pali Coast
  • Visiting Waimea Canyon, known as the "Grand Canyon of the Pacific"
  • Taking a helicopter tour of the island to see its stunning natural beauty from above
