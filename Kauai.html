Kauai Island

Kauai is the fourth largest island in Hawaii and is known for its lush landscapes, beautiful beaches, and outdoor activities.

Some popular attractions on Kauai include the Na Pali Coast, Waimea Canyon, Poipu Beach, and Hanalei Bay.
